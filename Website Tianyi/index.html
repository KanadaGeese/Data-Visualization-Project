About the NBA

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is a professional basketball league in North America composed of 30 teams (29 in the United States and 1 in Canada). It is widely considered to be the premier men's professional basketball league in the world.

NBA History

Founded in 1946, the NBA has grown into a global sports and media organization. Legendary players like Michael Jordan, LeBron James, Kobe Bryant, and Magic Johnson have left an indelible mark on the league and its fans.
